Dialect differences

The same clinical phrase, framed the way different communities actually speak.

The engine holds the clinical term fixed and re-traces the sentence across dialect and register variants of the surrounding syntax, so any probability shift is attributable to framing alone — extending the single syntax delta of the 2×2 matrix into a distribution across dialects.
